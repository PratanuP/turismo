Chatla Wetland

This is the beautiful Chatla Wetland. Chatla turns to a farming ground post monsoon and as the rain water fills it up, it smoothly turns out to be a fishing heaven. Located in Silchar in the Cachar district, it is a sight to behold just near the Silcoorie Tea Garden.
