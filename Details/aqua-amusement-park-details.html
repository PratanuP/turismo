Aqua Amusement Park

The Aqua Amusement Park opposite to the famed Marwari Dhaba near Kalyani Hospital, is a miniature aqua themed park , there is a small size pool with a few small slides put in, good for kids and the faint hearted ,an octopus shaped rain dance platform and a small waterfall . There is a trampoline, a sea-saws, a rope climbing stand and a few other games. Not much open space inside.Entry fees is quite high. Parking is available on the premises. Inside the aqua area there is a snacks bar with limited options. Separate changing rooms for male and female guests are available. The dhaba in the park serves tasty veg and non-veg food from the same kitchen. Service in the dhaba is a bit slow. Place is safe . Maintenance is not up-to the mark. The park is also available for events and private parties. Overall for a place like Silchar with limited options, a good place.
