Turismo is a Spanish term for ‘Tourism’. The key idea behind our Tourism is to Explore the untold parts of the Valley. Mostly what happens among the locals, when the mob has time to spare during vacation/weekends in search of amusement/pleasure, they visit malls and restros coz either they have nowhere else to go or unaware of the lesser known spots to visit. If you ask us, “What are we trying to uncover?”. We would simply tell that we are trying to let the people of the Valley and their friends of friends and family members to know more about the Barak Valley as well as explore it gradually via our website, TURISMO.
